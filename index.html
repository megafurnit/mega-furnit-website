Process

A practical path from sourcing brief to container loading.

The workflow is designed for B2B buyers who need reliable coordination, not a shopping cart.

  1. 01Requirement discussion

    Share your target market, product category, order size, quality level, and timeline.

  2. 02Product matching or development

    Review existing factory options or develop products from reference designs and specifications.

  3. 03Quotation and sample confirmation

    Compare suitable options, confirm materials, packaging, lead time, and sample direction.

  4. 04Production coordination

    Coordinate order details, factory communication, progress updates, and buyer requirements.

  5. 05Quality inspection and packaging

    Support QC coordination, packing review, carton marks, labels, and compliance-related details where applicable.

  6. 06Export documents and container loading

    Coordinate FOB shipment documents, loading plan, container utilization, and final handoff.

  7. 07Delivery support

    Support practical follow-up for importers, wholesalers, distributors, and regional buyers after shipment.
